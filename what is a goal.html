WHAT IS A GOAL ?

An aim or objective towards which an endeavor is directed.
A goal is simply a target which has to be accomplished.

IMPORTANCE OF GOALS IN LIFE

  • Goals give us a roadmap to follow.
  • Goals are a great way to hold ourselves accountable, even if we fail.
  • Goals provide direction, and focus. ...
  • Goals give a sense of personal satisfaction. ...
  • Goals help maintain motivation, especially during setbacks.
  • Goals allow clients to prioritize change. ...
  • Goals set a realistic timeline for goal accomplishment. ...
  • Goals provide a better understanding of expectations.

DIFFERENCE BETWEEN DREAM AND GOAL

A dream becomes a goal when action is taken towards achieving it.

A dream is a fantasy,

A goal is deciding what you want to accomplish.
